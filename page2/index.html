Hiking

Vasquez Rocks

April 08, 2019

An ethereal landscape fitting of another planet Vasquez Rocks is only an hour outside of LA but seems like a different world. The rocks emerge from the ground at unnatural angles, like books leaning in a row, creating complex rock formations that are quite easy for anyone to ascend. It makes the perfect day trip out of LA, and is relatively empty, at least during winter. Katie and I visited Vasquez rocks just before Christmas immediately after a rainstorm and were able to witness dramatic clouds pushed around by chilling winds as we scrambled up the rocky terrain.
