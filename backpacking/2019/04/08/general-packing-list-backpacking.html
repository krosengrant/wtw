General Packing List for Backpacking

April 08, 2019

Be sure to check both the weather and all of your gear before each trip.

Group Gear

  • First aid (allergy, Advil, blister pads, band-aids, etc.)
  • Emergency blanket(?)
  • Water filter
  • Iodine tablets
  • Flashlights
  • Tent (size will depend on the number and size of the people on the trip)
  • Tarp
  • Rope
  • Lighters and matches
  • Stove
  • Fuel
  • Pots & pans
  • Small shovel
  • Toilet paper

Individual Gear & Clothing

  • Sleeping pad
  • Sleeping bag
  • Swiss army knife
  • Wind/rain jacket
  • Fleece jacket
  • Extra fleece jacket (or hoodie)
  • Sweat pants
  • Thermal Leggings
  • Hiking pants
  • Shorts
  • Tops
  • Underwear
  • Multiple pairs of thick hiking socks
  • Sleeping socks
  • Beanie
  • Mug
  • Fork & spoon
  • Toothpaste
  • Sunscreen
  • Chapstick
  • Sun hat
  • Sunglasses
  • Small towel
  • Contact lenses & solution
  • Bathing suit (optional)
  • Plastic bag for wet swimsuit (optional)
  • Flip flops (optional)
